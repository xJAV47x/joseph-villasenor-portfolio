Case Study: NSWC Corona (NAVSEA)

Enterprise Data Platform & Governance

Microsoft Dataverse Power Apps DoD IL5 (FlankSpeed) RBAC/PIM

The Challenge

Identified a critical systemic failure in data management where hundreds of disparate SharePoint lists were being misused as relational databases. This "Wild West" environment led to massive data duplication, a total lack of command-level visibility, and an estimated 80% operational overhead due to manual data reconciliation.

Key Issue: Data Silos & Integrity

The lack of a centralized schema meant that leadership was making decisions based on fragmented and often contradictory project metrics.

Architectural Solution

1. Strategic Advocacy & Procurement

Successfully advocated at the PEO Digital (Washington Navy Yard) level to secure a dedicated environment. Presented the technical and business case for moving beyond SharePoint to a robust relational platform.

2. Dataverse Normalization

Architected a fully normalized relational data model in Microsoft Dataverse. This involved migrating legacy data from SharePoint into a structured schema with enforced referential integrity and authoritative data relationships.

3. Model-Driven UI & Automation

Designed and deployed a Model-Driven Power App as the primary interface. Integrated custom JSON formatting and GitHub-sourced Gantt chart components to provide real-time project visualization for Division and Branch heads.

The Impact

80%

Reduction in departmental data management overhead.

100%

Visibility for command leadership into project metrics.
