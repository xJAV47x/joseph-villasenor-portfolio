Case Study: JetZero Aerospace

Mission Operations Center (MOC)

Enterprise Architecture RF Communications Mission-Critical Systems Hardware Integration

The Challenge

The requirement was to design and deliver a mobile, mission-critical flight testing center capable of supporting real-time telemetry, simulator integration, and multi-display command operations for next-generation aerospace testing. This required a blend of high-end IT infrastructure and physical engineering to ensure stability and reliability in a mobile environment.

Architectural Solution

1. Mobile Infrastructure Design

Architected the entire mobile environment, including high-speed networking, server racks with vibration-dampening spring mounts, and redundant power solutions to support 50+ concurrent workstations.

2. RF & Communications Integration

Integrated omnidirectional antennas, RF communicators, receivers, and transmitters. Designed the cabling and wiring infrastructure with precise labeling for rapid component replacement and maintenance.

3. Physical Engineering & Hardening

Collaborated with builders to engineer structural supports, close gaps for environmental protection, and ensure the vehicle could withstand the rigors of flight testing operations.

The Impact

50+

Concurrent workstations supported in a mobile environment.

100%

Reliability during critical flight testing operations.
